How your mentor pay is calculated

You earn a share of what each of your mentees pays each month. The one twist: every invoice is split across two calendar months. Change the names, amounts, and invoice days below to match your own mentees and see what you'd be paid.

The rule, in one breath

Your rate is 35% → 50% → 60% over your first three months mentoring, then 60%. Each mentee's invoice is split by the day it falls on (using a fixed 30-day month): the part of the month still to come is paid that month, and the part already elapsed rolls into the next month.

elapsed = day ÷ 30 · paid this month = amount × (1 − elapsed) × rate · rolls to next month = amount × elapsed × rate
